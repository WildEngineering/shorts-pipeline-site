Shorts Pipeline

A personal tool for producing and publishing short educational engineering videos.

Shorts Pipeline is a private, single-user application built and operated by one person — the owner of the associated YouTube and TikTok channel, who is an aerospace engineer. It assists the owner in producing short, factual educational videos about engineering and aerospace, and in publishing them to the owner's own accounts.

What it does

  • The owner chooses every topic. Ideas originate from a human, not automated discovery.
  • For each topic, the tool drafts a script, generates a synthetic voice-over, selects licensed stock or public-domain footage, and assembles a vertical video.
  • Every video is reviewed by the owner for quality and factual accuracy before any upload.
  • Approved videos are uploaded to the owner's own YouTube and TikTok accounts.

Who it is for

This application has no external users. It authorizes only the owner's own accounts and acts only on those accounts. It collects no data about any other person and shares no data with third parties.
