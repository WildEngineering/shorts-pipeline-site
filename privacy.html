Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application "Shorts Pipeline" (the "App") handles information. The App is a personal, single-user tool operated by its owner (the "Owner") to produce and publish short videos to the Owner's own YouTube and TikTok accounts. The App has no other users.

1. Who operates this App

The App is operated privately by the Owner for the Owner's own use only. It is not a commercial service, is not offered to the public, and does not create accounts for anyone. Contact: aivideostom@gmail.com.

2. Information the App accesses

The App accesses only what is required to publish the Owner's own videos:

  • Authorization tokens for the Owner's own YouTube and TikTok accounts (OAuth access and refresh tokens), obtained with the Owner's consent.
  • Basic account information of the Owner's own account, as returned by the platform APIs, used only to perform uploads.
  • Video files and their metadata (titles, descriptions, captions, tags) created by the Owner.

The App does not access, request, or process data belonging to any other user.

3. How information is used

Information is used solely to upload the Owner's own approved videos to the Owner's own accounts, with accurate titles, descriptions, and tags. The App does not use any information for advertising, profiling, or analytics about other people.

4. Storage and security

  • All credentials and tokens are stored locally on the Owner's own computer, in a folder excluded from version control.
  • A local database stores only the Owner's own video identifiers and their processing status, to avoid uploading the same video twice.
  • No data is transmitted to any server other than the official APIs of the platforms used (Anthropic, ElevenLabs, Pexels, YouTube/Google, TikTok) to perform the App's functions.

5. Sharing with third parties

The App does not sell, rent, or share any information with third parties. Data is exchanged only with the official platform APIs listed above, strictly to perform the actions the Owner requests (generating a script, a voice-over, footage, or uploading a video).

6. Data retention and deletion

Tokens and local data remain on the Owner's computer until the Owner deletes them. The Owner can revoke the App's access at any time from the security settings of the relevant platform (Google/YouTube or TikTok), and can delete the local token files to remove all stored credentials.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly process any child's data.

8. Compliance

The App is designed to comply with the developer terms and policies of the platforms it uses, including the TikTok Developer Terms and the Google API Services User Data Policy. It requests only the minimum permissions needed and uses them only for their stated purpose.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the App changes. The effective date above indicates the latest revision.
